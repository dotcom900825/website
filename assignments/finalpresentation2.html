COGS 120/CSE170 FINAL PRESENTATIONS

On Friday 3/14, learn how 200 UCSD students see the future of mobile interaction. 5:00pm sharp @Multipurpose Room in the Student Services Center Link to Map

Overview

To start, each student team will present a 30 seconds summary of their work. After that, you'll get a chance to walk around and see posters and demos. Projects are related to one of these design topics:

  • Boundaries

  • Consequences of Ubiquity

  • Health

  • Cities

  • Design for Empowerment

  • Extending Memory

Schedule of Events

Our afternoon's schedule starts just before 4 pm:

  • 4:00pm : Students arrive to set up posters
  • 4:30pm : Refreshments and social mixer
  • 5:00pm : 30 Second Presentations
  • 5:45pm : Poster session and team demonstrations
  • 6:45pm : Results and Closing Remarks
