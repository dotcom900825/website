COGS 120/CSE 170 Intro to Human-Computer Interaction Design

Final Presentation

Friday, March 14, 5pm - 7pm

At Multipurpose Room in the UCSD Student Services Center

Come and check out how 200 UCSD students see the future of mobile interaction.

There will be 63 teams presenting their projects.

Agenda

  • 4:00pm --- Students arrive to set up posters
  • 4:30pm --- Refreshments and social mixer
  • 5:00pm --- Each team presents a 30-second pitch of their work.
  • 5:45pm --- Poster session and team demonstrations
  • 6:45pm --- Results and Closing Remarks

Project themes:Boundaries, Empowerment, Medicine, Health and Awareness, Cities, Consequences of Ubiquity, Community, Extending Memory
